Cultural Preservation

The Art of Tatreez

More than just clothing, Palestinian embroidery is a complex language of identity, resistance, and generational storytelling.

Every stitch carries the history of the land. We work directly with master artisans across the region to preserve this beautiful tradition. By adapting historical silhouettes for the modern wardrobe, we ensure fair wages, ethical sourcing, and the endurance of our cultural narrative for generations to come.
